Who We Are

Welcome to Regenerators Agro-Engineering Solutions. Our main aim is to empower Ethiopian farmers with innovative and sustainable agricultural solutions. Farm Hub Ethiopia is a registered company committed to revolutionizing agricultural practices in Ethiopia. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, we strive to bridge the gap between traditional farming methods and modern mechanized solutions. Our mission is to empower smallholder farmers with the tools, knowledge, and resources they need to increase productivity, adopt sustainable practices, and achieve food sovereignty. Through our Farm Hubs, we provide a one-stop-shop solution, connecting farmers with advanced machinery, certified inputs, and digital tools like the Last Mile App. By fostering collaboration, knowledge sharing, and market access, we aim to create a future where Ethiopian agriculture thrives, and farming communities prosper.
