Our Services

Tillage Equipment

Disk Harrow

The Disk Harrow is a robust secondary tillage implement designed to refine soil structure after primary plowing. It breaks clods, incorporates crop residues, and eliminates weeds, creating a fine, uniform seedbed ideal for sowing. It is compatible with 50HP tractors, it combines power and efficiency to meet the needs of Ethiopian smallholder farmers.

Rotary Tiller/Rotavator

The Rotary Tiller is a versatile tillage implement designed to prepare soil for planting by thoroughly mixing, aerating, and refining soil structure. It breaks hardpan, pulverizes clods, incorporates crop residues, and eliminates weeds, creating a uniform, fine-textured seedbed ideal for sowing. It is designed to be pulled by 50HP tractors, it combines power, precision, and efficiency to meet the needs of Ethiopian smallholder farmers. The unique design is ideal for seed bed preparation to improve the soil health and supplements in retaining soil moisture and increases porosity.

Cultivator

The Cultivator is designed for deep tillage and soil inversion. It is ideal for breaking up hard soil and preparing the land for planting.

Sub Soiler

Our Sub-Soiler Service tackles soil compaction by breaking up hardpan layers deep beneath the surface, improving root growth, water infiltration, and soil health. Designed for Ethiopian smallholder farms, this single-bottom sub-soiler is compatible with 50HP tractors, delivering targeted deep tillage without disturbing the topsoil. Ideal for fields suffering from waterlogging or poor drainage, this service revitalizes compacted soils for healthier, more productive crops.

Laser Land Leveler

Our Laser Land Leveler revolutionizes field preparation by delivering unparalleled precision in land leveling, ensuring optimal water distribution and uniform crop growth. It levels the field within a certain degree of the desired slope using a guided laser beam throughout the field. An unevenness of the soil surface has a significant impact on the germination, stand, and yield of crops.

Disk Ridger

Disc Ridger is specially designed implement for forming ridges or for making irrigation banks for flood irrigation by cutting and turning the soil in two opposite direction.

Planting Equipment

Super Seeder

Our Super Seeder revolutionizes planting by enabling direct seeding of crops like wheat and rice without prior tillage. This multi-purpose machine uses double notched disc furrow openers to create precise rows for uniform seed and fertilizer placement. It combines residue management, seeding, and fertilization in a single pass, saving time and protecting soil health.

Happy Seeder

Planting Service with Happy Seeder offers a soil conservation solution for sowing different crops without prior tillage, preserving soil health. This innovative technology cuts and incorporates crop residues into the soil while simultaneously planting seeds, ensuring optimal germination and reducing labor. It combines sustainability with efficiency, promoting conservation agriculture.

Raised Bed Planter

Our Raised Bed Multi-Crop Planter is designed for precise seeding of various crops on raised beds. The process of bed formation and seeding is completed in a single pass, improving drainage, reducing waterlogging, and optimizing soil warmth for quicker germination. This method allows for greater water conservation compared to flat and ridge planting methods, as it focuses irrigation on the furrows only, providing moisture to both adjacent beds. By integrating bed shaping, seed placement, and fertilizer application in one pass, this service ensures higher yields and efficient use of resources. Sowing can be done at varying row spacing of 20 cm to 40 cm, with sowing depths ranging from 5 cm to 10 cm.

Potato Planter

The Potato Planter revolutionizes potato production by delivering precise, efficient planting of potatoes in uniform rows. It creates optimal seed beds with integrated furrow systems, this versatile machine adapts to diverse soils, climates, and farm sizes. By automating planting and bed formation in a single pass in two rows, it eliminates manual labor, boosts yields, and reduces operational costs. It can also be used for other tuber crops by adjusting it.
