Who We Are

Farm Hub Ethiopia is a forward-looking agricultural company founded in 2023 to bridge the wide gap between mechanized and subsistence farming. Through our Farm Hubs, we provide scalable mechanization services, high-quality inputs, tailored digital solutions, and practical advisory services—all aimed at empowering smallholder farmers across Ethiopia.

We are more than a service provider; we are a system integrator, connecting innovation to implementation. As the implementation arm of a larger transformation ecosystem, we bring lab-tested solutions directly to farmers' fields. By fostering collaboration, knowledge sharing, and market access, we aim to create a future where Ethiopian agriculture thrives, and farming communities prosper.

The Problem We Are Solving

The global food system is in crisis. In Ethiopia, this challenge is felt acutely through:

  • Low Productivity: Yields are 30–50% below potential due to poor inputs, limited machinery, and outdated practices.
  • Weak Supply Chains: Farmers lack timely access to essential inputs and reliable markets.
  • Skills Gap: Only 18% of farmers receive formal training, blocking the adoption of better technologies.

Additionally, farm power availability in Ethiopia stands at just 0.05 kW/ha—ten times lower than in emerging economies. This perfect storm of challenges keeps smallholder farmers trapped in cycles of poverty while threatening national food security.

Our Approach - The Transformation Ecosystem

Our model is rooted in systems thinking and sustainable scaling:

1

Holistic Systems Solution

A one-stop-shop model connecting farmers to technologies, inputs and knowledge through an integrated network of Farm Hubs - the visible nodes of the system - ensuring local scalability by embedding solutions within existing farmer landscapes.

2

Closed-Loop Innovation

Sustainable/Market led Diffusion

Hubs operate as next-gen extension centers. Services are market-led and revenue-generating, ensuring long-term viability.
