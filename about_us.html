About Us

Farm Hub Ethiopia is a registered company committed to revolutionizing agricultural practices in Ethiopia. With a focus on innovation and sustainability, we strive to bridge the gap between traditional farming methods and modern mechanized solutions. Our mission is to empower smallholder farmers with the tools, knowledge, and resources they need to increase productivity, adopt sustainable practices, and achieve food sovereignty. Through our Farm Hubs, we provide a one-stop-shop solution, connecting farmers with advanced machinery, certified inputs, and digital tools like the Last Mile App. By fostering collaboration, knowledge sharing, and market access, we aim to create a future where Ethiopian agriculture thrives, and farming communities prosper.

Our Vision

To revolutionize the agricultural sector by empowering farmers with innovative agricultural machinery, inputs, and sustainable practices, creating a future where Ethiopian agriculture thrives, productivity soars, and communities prosper.

Our Mission

To enhance agricultural productivity, promote ecological agriculture, and stimulate local economic growth through the manufacturing and distribution of advanced agricultural machinery in Ethiopia. We are committed to providing farmers with the tools and knowledge they need to increase yields, reduce labor dependency, and adopt sustainable farming practices.

Interconnected System

Our solution is an integrated technology transfer system, with Farm Hubs serving as local hubs. These hubs provide farmers with access to equipment, digital tools, and resources to improve productivity, profitability, and resilience. By establishing a network of interconnected Farm Hubs, farmers can share best practices, access market information, and leverage collective bargaining power, driving wider systemic change. Ultimately, this network creates a virtuous cycle of innovation and collaboration, enhancing smallholder farmers' livelihoods and advancing food sovereignty across Ethiopia.
